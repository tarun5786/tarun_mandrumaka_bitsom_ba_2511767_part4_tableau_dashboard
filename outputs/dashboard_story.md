# Dashboard Story: Executive Sales Dashboard

## Executive Summary

This executive dashboard analyzes 4,200 retail orders across January 2024
to December 2025, covering ₹21.7 Crore in total sales at a 15.35% profit
margin. The dashboard was built for retail leadership to monitor sales
performance, profitability, customer segments, category performance,
shipping efficiency, discount impact, and return patterns.

The story the dashboard tells is simple and actionable:
> Technology is the profit engine. Furniture is destroying value.
> Heavy discounts are making orders loss-making. Fix these and the
> business improves significantly.

---

## What is Performing Well

### 1. Overall Growth is Positive
Sales grew 4.3% year-on-year from ₹10.62 Crore (2024) to ₹11.07 Crore
(2025). The business is expanding and total profit reached ₹3.33 Crore.

### 2. Technology is Highly Profitable
Technology generates 70.9% of all sales (₹15.39 Crore) at an 18.2%
margin, the highest of all categories. Copiers alone produced ₹73.10
Lakh in profit. This is the core profit engine of the business.

### 3. All Regions Are Stable
South leads with ₹64.69M in sales at 15.5% margin, but all four regions
operate within a tight 15.1–15.6% margin band. No region is in crisis.

### 4. Customer Segments Are Balanced
Home Office (₹74.50M), Consumer (₹71.89M), and Corporate (₹70.63M)
are all within 5% of each other, a sign of healthy diversification.

### 5. Same Day and First Class Shipping Perform Well
Same Day orders are delivered as promised (0 days). First Class
maintains mostly fast (1-2 day) delivery. Both modes are reliable.

---

## What is Underperforming

### 1. Furniture Margins Are Critically Low
All four Furniture sub-categories show red in the profitability chart:
- Overall Furniture margin: only 6.9% vs 18.2% for Technology
- Furnishings, Tables, Chairs, and Bookcases all underperform
- Many individual Furniture orders generate negative profit

### 2. Heavy Discounts Are Loss-Making
Orders with more than 30% discount generate -4.95% profit margin.
The staircase pattern is clear: every additional 10% discount removes
approximately 4-6 percentage points from margin.

### 3. Standard Class Shipping Is Slow
1,141 out of 2,435 Standard Class orders (47%) are delivered in
5-6 days falling into the "Slow" bucket. This is the most-used
shipping mode (58% of all orders), making it a widespread issue.

### 4. Furniture Return Rates Are Too High
Furniture East region: 9.77% return rate, more than double the
4.55% company average. All Furniture regions are elevated (6-10%).

---

## What Risks Are Visible

1. **Discount policy risk:** 64 orders with >30% discount are
   actively losing money. If this pattern grows, profitability erodes fast.

2. **Furniture value destruction:** Low margins + high returns + heavy
   discounts in Furniture create a compounding risk to overall profitability.

3. **Seasonal revenue dips:** August 2024 hit ₹6.30M the lowest month
   across both years. No mitigation strategy is visible in the data.

4. **Standard Class SLA risk:** 47% of the most-used shipping mode
   delivers slowly a customer satisfaction time bomb.

---

## What Opportunities Are Visible

1. **Double down on Technology:** 18.2% margin with ₹15.39 Crore in
   sales. Expanding Technology product range or pushing upsells here
   would directly improve overall profitability.

2. **Discount cap policy:** Capping discounts at 20% would immediately
   eliminate all loss-making orders and lift overall margin.

3. **Seasonal promotions:** Targeted campaigns in June-August could
   smooth the mid-year revenue dip visible in both years.

4. **Organic channel expansion:** Organic is the top acquisition channel
   (1,694 orders, 40.3%). Investing in SEO and content would amplify this.

---

## Recommended Business Actions

| Priority | Action | Expected Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| 🔴 Urgent | Cap discounts at 20% maximum | Eliminate -4.95% margin orders |
| 🔴 Urgent | Audit Furniture returns in East | Reduce 9.77% return rate |
| 🟠 High | Grow Technology sales mix | Leverage 18.2% margin category |
| 🟠 High | Fix Standard Class logistics SLA | Improve 47% slow delivery rate |
| 🟡 Medium | Seasonal promotions in June-August | Smooth revenue volatility |
| 🟡 Medium | Expand Organic channel investment | Build on top acquisition source |

---

## Limitations of the Analysis

1. No customer-level data cannot measure repeat purchase rates or CLV
2. No cost breakdown by product cannot identify specific cost drivers
3. Campaign channel has some null values channel ROI is incomplete
4. Geographic map view not built state-level drill-down not available
5. Data covers only 2024-2025 longer historical trends not visible

---

## How Charts Connect the Story

The dashboard is designed as a flow each chart answers the next question:

1. **Sales Trend** → Is the business growing? (Yes, 4.3% YoY)
2. **Regional Performance** → Where? (Everywhere equally)
3. **Category Profitability** → What's driving profit? (Technology yes, Furniture no)
4. **Discount Impact** → Why is Furniture red? (Heavy discounts)
5. **Shipping Performance** → How are we delivering? (Standard Class is slow)
6. **Customer Segment** → Who is buying? (All segments equally)
7. **Return Analysis** → What's coming back? (Furniture, especially East)

Each chart is a chapter. Together they tell one story.
